About Me

Red, oily hair awkwardly hangs over a full, time-worn face. Shuttered black eyes, set well within their sockets, watch delightfully over the homes they've nearly died for for so long. Fire has left a mark stretching from just under the right eye , running towards her right nostril and ending on her right nostril and leaves a fascinating memory of defended lands. The is the face of Juliet Massey, a true victor among humans.
